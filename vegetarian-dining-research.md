## Research Findings for: Dhermi, Himare, Jale Beach, Gjipe Beach, and Drymades

## Traditional Albanian Vegetarian Dishes

Based on Albanian cuisine research, here are traditional dishes that are naturally vegetarian or easily made vegetarian:

### Savory Dishes

1. **Byrek (Byreku)** - Flaky phyllo pastry pie filled with:
   - Spinach and feta (*byrek me spinaq*)
   - Cheese (*byrek me djathë*)
   - Potatoes (*byrek me patate*)
   - Leeks

2. **Fërgesë Verore (Summer Fërgesë)** - Vegetarian version of the national dish made with:
   - Green and red peppers
   - Skinned tomatoes
   - Onions
   - Sometimes eggs
   - Served as side dish

3. **Fasulle Plaqi** - Baked white kidney beans in earthen pot with:
   - Tomatoes
   - Onions
   - Peppermint
   - Oregano
   - Bay leaves

4. **Tarator** - Cold appetizer/soup for summer:
   - Cucumber
   - Garlic
   - Olive oil
   - Salt
   - Yogurt

5. **Speca të Ferguara** - Roasted peppers served with pite (flatbread)

6. **Japrak (Vegetarian version)** - Stuffed grape leaves with:
   - Rice
   - Chopped onions
   - Herbs
   - Ask for without meat

7. **Stuffed Peppers** - Green peppers filled with:
   - Rice
   - Vegetables
   - Herbs (ask for no meat)

### Salads

- **Sallatë Shqiptare (Albanian Salad)** - Tomato, cucumber, peppers, onions, olives, feta, dressed with olive oil and vinegar

### Breads & Sides

- **Bukë misri** - Cornbread
- **Pite** - Traditional flatbread

### Desserts (Most are vegetarian)

- **Baklava** - Walnut/hazelnut pastry in syrup
- **Tambëloriz** - Rice pudding with cinnamon
- **Trileçe** - Three-milk cake
- **Kadaif** - Shredded pastry with walnuts
- **Akullore** - Ice cream

## Important Tips for Vegetarian Dining in Albania

1. **Ask for "pa mish" (without meat)** - Many dishes can be made vegetarian on request

2. **Vegetarian-friendly phrases:**
   - "Jam vegeterian" = I am vegetarian
   - "Pa mish" = Without meat
   - "Ka mish në këtë?" = Does this have meat?

3. **Coastal restaurants** tend to have grilled vegetables and salads

4. **Breakfast options** are typically vegetarian: bread, cheese, olives, yogurt, jam, honey

## Restaurant Recommendations by Area

Note: Direct web access to specific restaurant details was limited. Based on general tourism patterns for the Albanian Riviera:

### Dhermi/Dhërmi
- Known for beachfront tavernas along the main beach road
- Look for places serving **fresh grilled vegetables**, **byrek**, **Greek-style salads**
- Ask locals for daily vegetarian specials

### Himare/Himarë
- Larger town with more variety
- Seafood restaurants typically offer excellent **vegetable meze**
- **Greek salads** and **tzatziki** widely available
- Beach clubs often serve **vegetarian wraps and pasta**

### Jale Beach
- Small beach with camping vibe
- Limited options - suggest bringing snacks or eating before arrival
- Beach bars may offer **sandwiches** (request vegetarian)

### Gjipe Beach
- Remote beach accessible by hiking
- Very limited food options
- **Bring your own food** recommended
- Sometimes small vendors sell snacks

### Drymades/Drimadhes
- Beach campground area
- Common room/restaurant at campsite
- **Pasta and grilled vegetables** usually available
- **Wood-fired pizzas** at some locations

## Sunset Dinner Spots

Generally recommended for views:
- **Dhermi**: Cliffside restaurants with sea views
- **Himare**: Beachfront restaurants facing west
- **Drymades**: Beach bars with sunset views over the water
